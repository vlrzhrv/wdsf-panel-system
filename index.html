📈 Historical Judge Analysis
How it works: Paste a WDSF competition slug (e.g. GrandSlam-Blackpool-Adult-Standard-65351) to scrape its official marks. After scraping several competitions, compute the Spearman correlation between all judge pairs. Judges who vote similarly get a lower independence score — this is used as Criterion F when building panels. The goal: select judges with diverse perspectives.
